01, Soundgarden - Never the Machine Forever (Down on the Upside 1996)

03. Green River - This Town (Dry as a Bone 1987)

06. Malfunkshun - My Only Fan (Another Pyrrhic Victory 1989)

07. Skin Yard - Open Fist (Hallowed Ground 1988)

08. Mudhoney - In 'n' Out of Grace (Superfuzz Bigmuff 1988)

09. Tad - Pork Chop (God’s Balls 1989)

10. Screaming Trees - I See Stars (Clairvoyance 1986)

11. Alice in Chains- It Ain't Like That (Facelift 1990)

12. Pearl Jam - Once (Ten 1991)

13. Pearl Jam - Hunted Down (Ten Club Single 2018)

14. Pearl Jam -Missing (Ten Club Single 2018)
